About Expense Tracker

Your ultimate tool for managing your finances effectively.

Introduction

Expense Tracker is designed to help you keep track of your daily expenses, manage your budget, and save money. Whether you're looking to get out of debt, save for a special purchase, or simply want to see where your money is going, our app provides the tools you need to stay on top of your finances.

Features

  • Track daily expenses easily
  • Set and manage budgets
  • Secure and private

How It Works

Using Expense Tracker is simple and straightforward:

  1. Add your income and expense entries.
  2. For income, use the "+" button; for expenses, use the "-" button.
  3. View the history of your expenses to monitor your spending.
  4. Adjust your spending and saving strategies accordingly.
